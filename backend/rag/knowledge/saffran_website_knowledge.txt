- Booking flow includes OTP email verification.
- OTP is valid for 10 minutes.
- Booking time validation range: 10:00 to 22:00.
- Past dates are not accepted.

About / Story
- Saffran is inspired by a family culinary tradition from the Middle East.
- Founder: Chef Ahmad Rashidi.
- Concept: blend of oriental tradition and Scandinavian elegance.
- Mission: share authentic flavors in Stockholm with quality ingredients and warm hospitality.
